# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Phoenix DevOps?

Phoenix DevOps is a DevOps automation company focused on implementing CI/CD security and automation strategies from day one. We provide comprehensive services to revolutionize software development and deployment with integrated security measures.

## What is your mission?

Our mission is to empower organizations with CI/CD security and automation strategy from day one, ensuring that security and automation are foundational elements of every DevOps pipeline rather than afterthoughts.

## What services do you offer?

We offer:
- CI/CD Pipeline Design and Implementation
- Security Integration (SAST, DAST, SCA, etc.)
- Automation Consulting
- DevSecOps Implementation

## What technologies do you use?

Our technology stack includes:
- CI/CD Tools: Jenkins, GitLab CI/CD, GitHub Actions, CircleCI, Azure DevOps
- Security Tools: SonarQube, OWASP ZAP, Snyk, Checkmarx, HashiCorp Vault
- Infrastructure Tools: Terraform, Ansible, Kubernetes, Docker, Cloud services
- Monitoring: Prometheus, Grafana, ELK Stack, Datadog, New Relic

## How do you approach security?

We follow a security-first approach with:
- Day One Security: Implementing controls from the start
- Continuous Security: Scanning and monitoring throughout CI/CD
- Compliance by Design: Building compliance into workflows

## What are your core principles?

- **Security-First Approach**: Integrate security early and continuously
- **Automation Excellence**: Automate infrastructure, pipelines, and monitoring
- **Scalability and Reliability**: Design for cloud-native, high availability solutions
