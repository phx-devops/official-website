Phoenix DevOps focuses on delivering high-impact services that follow DevOps best practices in CI/CD, security, infrastructure, monitoring, training, and implementation. Our packages are designed to help teams move faster, stay secure, and run reliable systems — whether you’re a non-profit (free of cost) or a for-profit organization.

## CI/CD Automation
We design and implement robust CI/CD pipelines that reduce manual effort and accelerate delivery.

- Pipeline as code with YAML and declarative workflows
- Automated build, test, and deployment stages
- Artifact management and secure pipeline gating
- Multi-environment promotion (dev → staging → prod)
- Rollback strategies and blue/green or canary deployments

## Security & DevSecOps
Security is integrated into every step of the pipeline to ensure safe deployments and compliance.

- Shift-left security with SAST, SCA, and secret scanning
- Runtime security and vulnerability monitoring
- Policy-as-code and automated compliance checks
- Secure credential management and secrets rotation
- Incident response integration and alerting

## Infrastructure Automation
We automate infrastructure provisioning and operations using infrastructure-as-code principles.

- Terraform, CloudFormation, and IaC best practices
- Modular infrastructure patterns and reusable components
- Environment consistency and drift detection
- Automated provisioning, scaling, and recovery
- Cost optimization and cloud governance

## Monitoring & Observability
Effective monitoring and observability ensure systems stay healthy and issues are detected early.

- Metrics collection and dashboards (Prometheus, Grafana)
- Log aggregation and analysis (ELK, Loki)
- Distributed tracing and performance insights
- Alerting with escalation and incident workflows
- Continuous improvement through metrics-driven feedback

## Training & Enablement
We provide training that empowers teams to own and extend DevOps practices.

- Hands-on workshops and role-based learning paths
- Documentation, runbooks, and playbooks aligned with your tooling
- Enablement for developers, SREs, and security teams
- Mentorship and continuous learning programs
- Knowledge transfer and internal coaching

## Implementation & Best Practices
Our implementation services are grounded in proven DevOps best practices.

- Assessment and roadmap planning
- Incremental delivery with prioritized outcomes
- Automation-first mindset across people, processes, and tooling
- Cross-team collaboration and shared ownership
- Ongoing support and continuous improvement
